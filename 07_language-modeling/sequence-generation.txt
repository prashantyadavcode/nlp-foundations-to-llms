Sequence Generation

Autoregressive generation loop:

Input
↓
Predict next token
↓
Append token
↓
Repeat

this is how chatgpt generates text


Sampling methods - 
modern LLMs don't always choose highest probability

Methods - greedy (highest probability), top-k (random among top k), top-p (nucleus sampling), temperature (control creativity)

Temperature -> low temp : more deterministic , high temp: more creative/random
