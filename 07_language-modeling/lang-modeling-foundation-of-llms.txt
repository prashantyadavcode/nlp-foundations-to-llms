Language Modeling - Foundations of LLMs

language modeling is the core idea behind modern NLP and LLMs
Goal - predict language patterns
modern systems like - GPT, BERT and Llama are fundamental language models

What is Lang. Model? - it learnes probability of word sequences
eg - 'I love machine ___' -> 'learning' it predicts with high probability

Core Goal - given previous words, predict next word.

Sequence Probability - lang. model estimates P(w1, w2, w3, .. wn)
                     - probability of an entire sentence

Chain Rule of Probability
- lang. models decompose sequence probability
- means each word depends on previous words
