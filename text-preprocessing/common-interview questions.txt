Ans -
1 - Because raw text is noisy and inconsistent.

Reduce vocabulary size
Improve model accuracy

2 - Because it may damage semantics:

studies → studi

Modern NLP prefers lemmatization/subword tokenization.


3 - Important modern NLP question.

Transformers often skip:

Stemming
Lemmatization
Heavy stopword removal

Because transformer tokenizers learn contextual meaning.
