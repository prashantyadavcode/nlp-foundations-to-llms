2. Encoder and decoder (most imp.)
encoder - understand input text, used in BERT, RoBERTa
decoder - generate text, used in GPT, ChatGPT
encoder-decoder - Input -> Output transformation, used in translation, summarization, T5


3. Input Embeddings - words converted into vectors
eg - 'I love AI' -> [[0.12, 0.55, ...], [0.92, 0.11, ...], [0.45, 0.77, ...]]


Problem? transformers ignore order
self-attention sees all words simultaneously
without extra info - 'dog bites man' and 'man bites dog' looks similar


4. Positional encoding - adds word position info.
why positional encoding? transformers do not inherently understand sequence order
positional encoding injects - token postion information

Intuition - each position gets a unique mathematical pattern

model learns - relative and absolute positions
