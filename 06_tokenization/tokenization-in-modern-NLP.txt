Text → Tokens → IDs → Embeddings → Model

Types of Tokenization
- Word - hugh vocab, doesn't work with unseen words (OOV), memory issues
- Character - sequence becomes huge, no OOV problem , tiny vocab
- Subword - best balance between word and character. 

Byte Pair Encoding (BPE) - modern tokenizer
- used in - GPT models, many LLMs
- How BPE works? - starts with characters, then repeatedly merge frequent pairs.
